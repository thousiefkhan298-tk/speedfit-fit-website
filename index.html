❓ FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

How quickly will I see results?+

Most clients notice visible changes within 6–10 days. Significant transformations typically happen within 8–12 weeks depending on your starting point and consistency level.

Do I need gym access?+

Not necessarily. I design programs for full gym, dumbbells-only and bodyweight training. During your initial call we'll assess what you have and design accordingly.

Can you create meal plans with Indian food?+

Yes — this is one of my specialties. All meal plans are built around home-cooked Indian food: dals, paneer, soya chunks, eggs, rice and rotis. No supplements or imported foods required.

How does remote coaching work?+

100% via WhatsApp. You receive your training plan, nutrition guide and a daily check-in system. I review your workout videos, track your weight and adjust your plan weekly.

What is the program cost?+

Pricing depends on duration and goal. Book a free 15-minute call and I'll give you a complete breakdown based on your exact requirements — no commitment needed.

Is coaching only for people in Chennai?+

No — I coach clients across India remotely via WhatsApp. If you're in Chennai, in-person sessions can also be arranged at my training facility.
